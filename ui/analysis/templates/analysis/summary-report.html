Most Frequent Speakers by Network

Verbosity

We define verbosity as the average number of words a speaker says before finishing their statement or being interrupted by another speaker. Lower verbosity means the conversations are quicker and/or more frequently interrupted, while higher verbosity means the conversations are slower and more drawn out. We have excluded hosts/anchors from the analysis for these graphs.
